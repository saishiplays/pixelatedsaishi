About Me

Get to know the artist.

Hi, I am Saishi!

PIXEL ARTIST | CONCEPT ARTIST | CONTENT CREATOR

I'm a pixel artist and gamer who enjoys creating art. I've been part of the creative industry since 2017, working on original ideas and imaginative designs.

My work is inspired by games and world-building, where I transform ideas into detailed pixel art. I particularly enjoy designing fictional characters and creatures, with a strong preference for creating large-scale, imposing designs. I continuously explore different art styles to expand my creative range and refine my visual storytelling.

MetaHeadverse Archead

FOUNDER OF METAHEADVERSE

MetaHeadverse is my ongoing alternate universe project that I founded and started building last year, and it is still actively expanding. It serves as a creative space where I explore worldbuilding, character design, and visual storytelling in a connected system. This project pushes me to refine my style, test concepts, and build a consistent universe that grows with every addition.

Pixels Online UGC Creator

PIXELS UGC ENTHUSIAST

It has been six months since I began creating UGC, and what started as a hobby has grown into a meaningful creative pursuit. As of April 2026, I have achieved 56 approved UGC pieces. Showcasing my artistic skills within one of the largest Web3 games has become one of my greatest passions, motivating me to continue building, experimenting, and expanding my creative universe.
